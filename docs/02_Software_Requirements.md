# Software Requirements Specification (SRS)

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Draft

---

# 1. Project Overview

AADI SEEDS ERP is a complete Retail, Nursery, Inventory, CRM, Accounting and Services Management System designed specifically for:

- Seed Stores
- Nursery Businesses
- Garden Centers
- Agricultural Retailers
- Landscaping Businesses

The ERP will simplify daily operations, improve inventory management, reduce manual work, and support future business growth.

---

# 2. Project Objectives

The software should:

- Reduce manual work
- Improve inventory accuracy
- Reduce billing time
- Maintain complete customer history
- Manage suppliers
- Track inventory automatically
- Support optional GST billing
- Generate business reports
- Work without internet (Offline First)
- Synchronize automatically when internet is available

# 4. Business Rules

## GST

- GST Invoice is Optional.
- Regular Bill is the default invoice type.

## Pricing

The ERP must support:

- Retail Price
- Wholesale Price
- Dealer Price
- Custom Price

## Inventory

Stock can change only through:

- Purchase
- Sale
- Sales Return
- Purchase Return
- Stock Adjustment
- Damaged Stock
- Opening Stock

Manual stock editing should not be allowed.

## Customers

Customer information should be stored permanently.

Each customer should have:

- Purchase History
- Credit History
- Sales History

## Barcode

Products may:

- Have manufacturer barcode
- Use system-generated barcode
- Have no barcode initially
